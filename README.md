# HRIS Employee Lifecycle Management System

### Business Analysis & QA Case Study (2026 | Self-Initiated)

---

## Project Overview

This project is a **real-world simulation of an HRIS (Human Resource Information System)** designed for a mid-sized IT services organization transitioning from manual HR operations to a centralized digital platform.

The implementation follows a **multi-phase rollout approach** to ensure smooth adoption, minimize risk, and deliver incremental business value.

The system focuses on **end-to-end employee lifecycle management**, covering onboarding, leave management, employee self-service, and exit processes.

---

## Project Objectives

* Digitize and standardize HR operations
* Reduce manual effort and process inefficiencies
* Improve data accuracy and accessibility
* Enable employee self-service capabilities
* Enhance visibility and decision-making through dashboards

---

## Phase-wise Implementation Plan

### Phase 1: Core HR Operations (MVP)

#### Modules:

**1. Onboarding Management (Post-Selection)**

* E-form based employee data capture
* Document submission and approval workflows
* Role and department allocation
* System access initiation via notifications
* Checklist-based onboarding tracking

**2. Employee Leave Management System**

* Leave application and tracking
* Multiple leave types (Sick, Casual, Earned)
* Manager approval workflows
* Real-time leave balance validation
* Employee dashboard and leave history

#### Objective:

* Digitize high-frequency HR processes
* Reduce manual dependency on HR
* Establish system adoption and process standardization

---

### Phase 2: Employee Self-Service & Information Hub

#### Modules:

**1. HR Policies & Benefits + Employee e-Forms**

* Centralized policies and benefits dashboard
* Employee data update via e-forms:

  * Bank Details Change
  * Address Change
  * Benefits Application
  * General Query Submission
* Workflow-based approvals and notifications

**2. Information Dashboard**

* Company announcements
* Policy updates
* Organizational news
* Alerts and notifications

#### Objective:

* Enable employee self-service
* Improve communication transparency
* Reduce HR operational workload

---

### Phase 3: Advanced HR Lifecycle Management

#### Modules:

**1. Leave of Absence (LOA) / Maternity**

* Extended leave workflows
* Policy-based validations
* Automated approvals

**2. Appraisal & Promotion**

* Performance dashboards
* Promotion workflows
* Manager evaluations and approvals

**3. Exit Management**

* Resignation submission via e-forms
* Exit clearance workflows
* Knowledge transfer tracking
* Final settlement notifications

#### Objective:

* Enable complete employee lifecycle management
* Support strategic HR decision-making
* Improve governance and compliance
