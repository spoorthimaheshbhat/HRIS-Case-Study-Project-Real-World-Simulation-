# AS-IS Process – Employee Onboarding (Functional View)

### HRIS Case Study Project (Phase 1)

---

## Process Objective

To onboard newly selected candidates into the organization by:

* Collecting employee details and documents
* Verifying submitted information
* Assigning roles and departments
* Enabling system access and readiness for joining

---

## Process Actors & Responsibilities

| Actor                | Responsibility                                    |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| HR Team              | Initiate onboarding, collect and verify documents |
| Candidate (New Hire) | Provide personal details and required documents   |
| Hiring Manager       | Confirm role, team, and reporting structure       |
| IT/Admin             | Provision system access and credentials           |

---

## Current Workflow (AS-IS Process)

### Trigger:

Candidate is selected and accepts the offer

---

### Step 1: Onboarding Initiation

* HR sends onboarding email to candidate
* Email includes:

  * List of required documents
  * Forms to be filled (attached or shared separately)

---

### Step 2: Data & Document Submission

* Candidate shares:

  * Personal details (via email/Word/PDF forms)
  * Documents (ID proof, certificates, bank details)
* Submission is done via email attachments

---

### Step 3: Document Verification

* HR manually reviews submitted documents
* If incomplete:

  * HR requests re-submission via email
* No structured validation checklist

---

### Step 4: Role & Department Allocation

* Hiring manager confirms:

  * Team assignment
  * Reporting manager
* Information shared via email or internal communication

---

### Step 5: System Access Request

* HR raises request to IT/Admin via email
* Includes:

  * Employee details
  * Role-based access requirement

---

### Step 6: Access Provisioning

* IT/Admin creates:

  * Email ID
  * System credentials
* Credentials shared via email

---

### Step 7: Onboarding Confirmation

* HR confirms onboarding completion
* Candidate joins organization

---

## Business Rules (Current State)

* All required documents must be submitted before joining
* Document verification must be completed by HR
* System access is provided only after role confirmation
* No standardized format for data collection

---

## Process Characteristics

| Aspect        | Current State                            |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Workflow Type | Manual (Email-driven)                    |
| Data Capture  | Unstructured (documents/forms via email) |
| Validation    | Manual                                   |
| Tracking      | No centralized tracking                  |
| Notifications | Email-based                              |
| Visibility    | Limited                                  |
| Audit Trail   | Not maintained                           |

---

## Functional Gaps Identified

### Workflow Gaps

* No structured onboarding workflow
* No defined stages or status tracking

---

### Data Capture Gaps

* Inconsistent formats for employee data
* High chances of missing or incorrect information

---

### Validation Gaps

* No automated validation or checklist enforcement
* Manual document verification

---

### Coordination Gaps

* Multiple dependencies (HR, Manager, IT) handled via email
* Lack of synchronization across teams

---

### Tracking & Visibility Gaps

* No real-time onboarding status
* HR cannot track progress centrally

---

### Communication Gaps

* No automated notifications or reminders
* High dependency on follow-ups

---

## Business Impact

* Delays in onboarding completion
* Increased manual effort for HR and IT
* Risk of missing or incorrect employee data
* Poor onboarding experience for new hires
* Lack of process transparency

---

## Summary

The current onboarding process is **manual, fragmented, and highly dependent on email communication**, leading to inefficiencies, delays, and lack of visibility.

A **centralized, workflow-driven HRIS solution** is required to standardize onboarding, improve coordination, and enhance user experience.
