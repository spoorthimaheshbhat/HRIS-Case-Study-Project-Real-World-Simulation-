# Phase 1 Overview – Core HR Operations (MVP)

---

## Overview

Phase 1 focuses on digitizing **high-frequency HR operations**, specifically **Onboarding Management** and **Employee Leave Management**, to reduce manual effort and establish a foundation for HRIS adoption.

This phase targets immediate operational inefficiencies caused by manual processes such as email-based approvals and spreadsheet tracking.

---

## Objectives

* Digitize onboarding and leave management processes
* Reduce dependency on manual HR intervention
* Improve process efficiency and turnaround time
* Enable real-time visibility into employee data
* Establish structured workflows and system adoption

---

## Modules in Scope

### 1. Onboarding Management (Post-Selection)

#### Description:

Handles the onboarding process for newly selected candidates, ensuring structured data capture, document verification, and smooth transition into the organization.

#### Key Features:

* E-form based employee data capture
* Document submission and approval workflows
* Role and department allocation
* Onboarding checklist tracking
* Automated notifications for task completion

---

### 2. Employee Leave Management System

#### Description:

Provides a centralized system for employees to apply, track, and manage leave requests with automated approval workflows.

#### Key Features:

* Leave application and tracking
* Multiple leave types (Sick, Casual, Earned)
* Automated approval workflow (Employee → Manager)
* Real-time leave balance validation
* Leave history and audit tracking
* Employee dashboard view

---

## Key Problems Addressed

### Onboarding:

* Manual data collection via emails and documents
* Lack of structured onboarding workflow
* Delays in document verification and system access

### Leave Management:

* Email-based leave requests
* No real-time leave balance tracking
* Delayed approvals and lack of visibility
* High dependency on HR for updates

---

## Proposed Solution

* Implement a centralized HRIS platform
* Automate onboarding workflows using e-forms and task tracking
* Enable system-driven leave application and approval processes
* Provide dashboards for real-time visibility and tracking
* Introduce automated notifications to reduce delays

---

## Stakeholders Involved

* HR Team
* Employees
* Reporting Managers
* IT/Admin Team

---

## Expected Outcomes

* Reduced manual effort in HR operations
* Faster onboarding and leave approval processes
* Improved data accuracy and consistency
* Enhanced employee experience
* Increased transparency and process visibility

---

## Dependencies

* Availability of HR policies and onboarding templates
* Stakeholder availability for validation
* Initial employee data readiness

---

## Assumptions

* Users have basic system knowledge
* Approval hierarchy is predefined
* HR policies are standardized

---

## Success Criteria

* Successful onboarding workflow execution without manual intervention
* Reduction in leave approval turnaround time
* Increased employee adoption of the system
* Accurate and centralized employee data management
